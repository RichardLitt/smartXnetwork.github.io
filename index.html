How it works

Set up your smartID

Get your secure and simple smart identity with built in digital signatures based on PKI technology. Each individual is issued a unique public-private keypair that acts as their identifier and password for authentication. Each entry in the smartX database is signed by writing peer's corresponding keypair. Complete your profile including social handles to showcase your true self.

Get verified and claim your smartCash

Once you've created your account you need to get verified from an existing verified peer to claim your smartCash and start participating in network activities. Monetary supply is fixed in smartX network but unlike other hard cryptocurrencies it's given directly to people and only minted at a transparent rate when a new verified peer joins the network. Early you join more you get.

Start verifying others and participating in markets

Now you can start verifying your friends and keep earning 2.5% per verification (verify only those you personally know else you risk losing all your wealth). Soon you'll be able to create, explore and trade tokens for any kind of digital good - from tweets to videos to memes - with built in incentive and compliance rules.

The system simply relies on incentive/mechanism design principles to function.
